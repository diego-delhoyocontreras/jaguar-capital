Careers at Jaguar Capital

Jaguar Capital is always keen to hear from exceptionally bright applicants (either experienced or graduates) who are ambitious, entrepreneurial, relish a challenge and have a keen sense of adventure by wanting to work in the fast growing emerging markets. We value mental agility, innovative thinking, and the ability to work collaboratively. Teamwork is as important to us as individual excellence.

We are particularly looking for graduates from a wide variety of disciplines. Although a background or previous experience in finance is not necessary, you must be numerate and keen on furthering your education. We can also provide work experience to penultimate and final year university students who are highly numerate.

For career opportunities with Jaguar Capital, please send a CV and covering letter to careers@jaguarcapital.com
