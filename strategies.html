Our Fundamental Funds

Our Quantitative Funds

Global Trend-Following Equity Fund

International fund trading optimised Factor-Based Models. Factor models may incorporate fundamental information, including determinants of “value” versus “growth” stocks such as cash flow multiples, sales to price ratios, or P/E to earnings growth figures.

Global Statistical Arbitrage Fund

Selective portfolio trading a mean-reverting trend of the values of similar baskets of assets based on historical trading relationships. This strategy reduces the fund's net exposure to risk factors (Market Neutral trading) and boosts risk-adjusted returns.

Global Macro Strategies Fund

Focused on using predictive data science to forecast the macroeconomic environment, often concentrated on currencies or major interest-rates moves. We combine this knowledge with fundamental equity research to build superior portfolios.
